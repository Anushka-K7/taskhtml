Paris, the capital city of France, is one of the most popular travel destinations in the world. Known as the "City of Light," Paris is famous for its rich history, iconic landmarks like the Eiffel Tower, and its artistic heritage.

The Iconic Glass Pyramid:The Louvre Museum

The Louvre Museum is the world's largest art museum and a historic monument in Paris. Home to over 35,000 works of art, including the famous Mona Lisa and the Venus de Milo, the Louvre is a must-visit for art and history lovers. Its iconic glass pyramid entrance leads to a treasure trove of masterpieces spanning centuries and cultures, making it one of the most visited museums in the world.
